Kyle McIntire

20 year old Exercise Science major, Aspiring Personal Trainer, and Men's Physique Competitor

Can you tell us a little about yourself?

My name is Kyle McIntire. I am an Exercise Science major at Slippery Rock University. I am very passionate about health and fitness and am pursuing a career in helping people change their lives for the better.

What does your workout routine look like?

My routine is a six day a week split tailored towards my goals of competing in an upcoming men's physique competition. Day 1 is a back and bicep workout. Day 2 is chest and triceps. Day 3 is shoulders. Day 4 is chest and back. Day 5 is legs. Day 6 is arm and shoulders. I also throw some abdominal work throughout the week as well.

Which products have had the most significant impact on your fitness journey?

C4 Pre-workout to give me the energy boost I need to get out of bed at 6 every morning to go lift. Isolate Protein Powder from Six Star because it delivers 30 grams of protein per scoop for only 150 calories and is very tasty when cold. Wrist Wraps because I have dealt with injuries throughout my fitness journey so protecting my joints is very important. Insoles for my shoes so that I can keep my legs, hips, and feet healthy while I do my cardio. Lifting belt to assist with heavy compound movements and to protect my lower back from injury.
